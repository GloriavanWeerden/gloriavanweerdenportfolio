Projects During my time at Wilfrid Laurier University and the Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan, I had the pleasure of participating with great teams on many projects. Here are a curated list of my favourites to best displays what I can bring to your team.

Integrative Conservation Clinic

This was a Capstone project completed over the full final year for the William & Mary University in Virginia. With a team of eight, we worked with Dr. Alexandra Sabo to create a platform for professionals in the conservation industry.

Case Study

Diamond Ride

Diamond Ride is an app designed for the students of Laurier Brantford. While it might be the Golden Hawks second home, Brantford is not included in many bus or train routes keeping students isolated. With this app, we hope to not only provide ease of transport but to create a stronger community.

Project Details

Rathfon Design

This could be called my take on a classic. Rathfon Machine and Tool was the name of my family's machining business up until 2018. The original logo was a clip art heron. In a Graphic Design course at Laurier, I created a new branding guide for the business for a more professional and modern image.
